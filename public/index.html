Yötaivaan tähti

Premiere: TBA
Project type: Client
Role: Composer
Lyrics: Vesa Sorola
Choir arrangement: Ilkka Vesala

Yötaivaan tähti is a 17-minute composition. It consists of six parts that describe the life of an archetypal Finnish man.

Vesa Sorola wrote the lyrics, I composed the music and Ilkka Vesala will arrange the piece for a male choir. Ilomantsin mieslaulajat will premiere the piece sometime in the future.

Häämatka – Honeymoon

Premiere: June 5, 2019
Project type: Client
Role: Composer, actor
Lyrics: Lauri Jänis

Honeymoon is a play premiered by Teatteri Koli in 2019.

I composed the music for the play together with my wife Elli. The play contains several songs that feature lyrics by the playwright Lauri Jänis.
